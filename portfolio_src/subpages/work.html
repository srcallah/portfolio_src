Sam Callahan is a graphic designer currently living and working in New Haven, CT. She received her MFA at the Yale School of Art in 2023.

ACTION REQUIRED
Exhibition Identity, 2021

Custom glyphs, gallery signage, vinyl and brochure designed in collaboration with Junyi Shi for the Yale School of Art First Year MFA Show.

Wish you were hear
SSTV Transmissions, 2022

Wish you were hear stitches together a series of images and writings that were transmitted using using a handheld two-way radio. The images are encrypted into a series of tones and decrypted using SSTV software.

Pacifico Silano for The Yale School of Art Photo Lecture Series
Multimedia announcement, 2023

Silano is a lens-based artist that documents the print culture of the LGBTQ+ identity. For the lecture announcement, I photographed images of 70s male erotica located in the Yale's Archives and Manuscripts and designed a set of transparency film to be placed in an analog microfiche reader.

Shen Xin for The Yale School of Art Photo Lecture Series
Multimedia announcement, 2022

Inspired by their installation "The Earth Turned Green": a projection of video captures of seasonal light mapped onto metal folding chairs with applied vinyl.
